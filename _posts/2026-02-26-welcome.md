This is your first blog post! 

## How to create more posts:
1. Create new files in the `_posts/` folder
2. Follow the naming format: `YYYY-MM-DD-post-title.md`
3. Add front matter (the section between the `---` lines)
4. Write your content in Markdown

Posts are sorted by date and automatically appear on your site.
